# Governance Frameworks
Contains AI governance framework documents that establish the policies, processes, roles, responsibilities, and oversight mechanisms used to govern AI technologies throughout their lifecycle. These frameworks define how organizations evaluate, approve, monitor, and manage AI systems while addressing governance, risk, compliance, security, privacy, and Responsible AI requirements. Documents may include governance structures, operating models, risk tiering methodologies, approval workflows, control requirements, monitoring expectations, and mappings to industry standards, regulatory requirements, and recognized frameworks such as NIST AI RMF, NIST 800-53 Rev. 5, and OECD AI Principles.
